About Us

Great Pacific Group LLC is a small business providing medical staffing services to federal agencies. We specialize in matching qualified professionals with the unique needs of government healthcare facilities.

We are based in Hawaii and serve contracts nationwide. Our team finds and places candidates for even the hardest-to-fill positions — efficiently and reliably.

Capabilities

We provide staffing solutions for all major clinical and administrative roles, including:

  • Physicians: Primary care, psychiatry, specialty care
  • Nurses: RNs, LPNs, nurse practitioners
  • Allied Health: Therapists, techs, pharmacists
  • Admin & Support: Medical admin, coders, clerks

We staff for both short-term and long-term needs — no emergency response. We’re built to solve your toughest personnel challenges.
