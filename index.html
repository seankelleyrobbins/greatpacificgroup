Great Pacific Group is an SBA-certified small business specializing in medical and administrative staffing for the Federal Government. We are a one-stop shop for agencies that need to quickly and reliably fill both short- and long-term positions across the country. Our core mission is to support federal operations with qualified, cleared, and ready-to-work personnel — especially in healthcare and mission-critical support roles. We understand the urgency and complexity of government staffing needs, and we pride ourselves on delivering responsive, compliant solutions without compromise. Whether it’s a single urgent fill or an ongoing contract requiring full-time professionals, Great Pacific Group delivers. We partner with government clients and small business subcontractors to ensure coverage that’s fast, dependable, and tailored to the mission.
