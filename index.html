About Us

Great Pacific Group provides specialized medical staffing services to the U.S. Federal Government. We are committed to delivering high-quality healthcare professionals to meet the critical needs of government healthcare facilities across the nation.

Our Capabilities

  • Physicians & Surgeons: Primary care, emergency medicine, specialists, and surgical staff
  • Nursing & Allied Health: RNs, NPs, PAs, therapists, and technicians
  • Behavioral & Mental Health: Psychologists, LCSWs, counselors, and psychiatrists
  • Administrative & Support Roles: Medical coders, case managers, schedulers, and administrators
  • Custom/Niche Staffing: Hard-to-fill or mission-critical positions tailored to federal agency needs
